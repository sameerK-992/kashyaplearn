What we do

Launched in 1989 we were dubbed a new computer program that could potentially ‘transform’ the industry. Today we operate in 25 countries and have thousands of customers worldwide. As the world’s market leader in Management and Dispatch Systems, over 1 million jobs are booked through our software everyday. My Taxi Ride Dispatch System International has established itself as the largest manufacturer and supplier of dispatch systems in the World, with recognition from the Motorola Corporation, awarding My Taxi Ride Dispatch System ‘Best use of an application in United States’.
