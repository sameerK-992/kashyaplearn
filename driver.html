My Taxi Ride Dispatch System’s Driver Application is an excellent app and a fully customizable White Label Product, which can be branded with the logo or name of your company. This app is designed to receive and manage booking, either from the dispatch company, driver or from the passenger. With this user-friendly Driver App, you can get it touch with the potential passenger directly from your Smartphones and save the time, without even losing another booking to a busy phone signal.

Attention grabbing App

This driver app is designed particularly with a user-experience interface, which runs on iPhone and Android phones. The beautifully designed map based menu screen has amazingly cool screen movements and buttons that attract the attention of the users while booking.

Rich Feature

My Taxi Ride Dispatch System’s Driver app has all the features you are seeking in an app, such as advanced booking options, track location and many more. The rich features of the app allow the users to get all information about their booking without any annoyance.

In-app navigation

With our in-app navigation feature, reaching to any destination is no more a difficult task for drivers. By using the navigation/GPS of smartphone or tablet, this application offers in-app direction navigation and street mapping to analyze pickup and drop-off location.

Mobile-Dispatching

At the times when no dispatcher is presented on the duty, some drivers are permitted to dispatch straightforwardly from the app. The feature is best used in combination with MTR auto dispatching feature, where all app booking and online booking are automatically dispersed to the driver.

With so many awe-inspiring features, downloading Driver App is certainly the best way to augment your driving business successfully.
