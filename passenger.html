Passenger's App

My Taxi Ride Dispatch System’s Passenger apps allow you to book travels, trips and make payments anytime and anywhere. This app is designed, keeping in mind the interest and benefit of the customer. It enables passengers to book a taxi and track its location on their Smartphones, whenever and wherever they want.

White Label Solutions

Our white label solutions give your passengers what they want, without endangering your brand. This solution allows you to brand your app with your logo and name to showcase the amendment of leading edge technology of your company. Easy Booking Procedure

Whether you need to contact driver, need large vehicle or seek the most convenient method to pay, the My Taxi Ride Dispatch System’s passenger app can help you in getting appropriate solutions of all these queries. With the feature of multi-language, booking a taxi is not a hard nut to crack anymore! The booking process is quite easy and can be done within a few seconds.
